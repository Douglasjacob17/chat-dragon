Spartan Pokémon League

Hello! Are you a Pokémon trainer looking for a great community for plenty of battles, trading, and fun people?

Then Spartan Pokémon League may be the place for you! We are a Pokémon battle community server on Discord, looking to expand our trainer count!

We have:
|| GIVEAWAYS
Giveaways consisting of HAs, Aprimon, mega stones, berries, and other rare items and Pokémon! Legendaries and mythical Pokémon are occasionally given away, along with the occasional shiny! Mystery gift codes and Pokécord items are also given away!

|| PARTNERSHIPS/SPONSORSHIPS
"Member of the Week" gets a free week-long advert in our sponsor channel, promoting their website, TouTube channel, social media, Discord server, or whatever!

|| TOURNAMENTS
Once a month, we host a server-wide tournament with one winner! Top three get a nice prize! Winner goes up against the reigning Champion for a stab at the title! May the best trainer win!

|| GYMS/E4
One feature we are proud of here at SPL is our establishment of the gyms. One for each type, there are 18 in total. Each leader has differing levels of battling ability, so you can grow stronger with each battle! Collect 8 or more and you can challenge the Elite 4!
Upon defeating at least 8 gyms, you can go up against the Elite 4, an elite group of trainers with very strong battle skills and teams! Defeat them, and you face off against the reigning Pokémon Champion!

|| PERKS
The more active you are in our community, the better it is for you! At level two, you gain access to the giveaways channel! Level five gets you access to the server's official breeding and training market. Ten get you permission to use an Ultra Beast in League battles. More levels gets you better things, so on and so forth.
