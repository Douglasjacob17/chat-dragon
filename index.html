The Faction

Apocolypta Astris

Author : Sammy Rheatt

I awake to the sound of a throbbing, droning noise. Rubbing my eyes, it takes a second to realize what the sound is. Red alert, I think to myself.What now? Knowing my duty, I leap out of bed and hurriedly dress myself in my blues, standard dress for Galavation Stellar Navy. Now the alert is fully realized, and my head is throbbing. Running out of my quarters, I stumble on a book on the floor, stubbing my toe. "Gah! What a start." I say with disdain.
