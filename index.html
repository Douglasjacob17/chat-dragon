The Faction

Apocolypta Astris

Author : Sammy Rheatt

I awake to the sound of a throbbing, droning noise. Rubbing my eyes, it takes a moment to realize what the sound is. Red alert, I think to myself, Battle stations. Soon the voice over the intercom says, RED ALERT, BATTLE STATIONS. Instinctively, I leap out of bed and prepare to throw on my blues, when I survey my current location. The shiny, smooth chrome-color metal that curves around the room, the hard corners with no edge, the 5 centimeter thick bed, and the hum coming from the doorway all point to one thing. The brig.

Yep, evidently I forgot that I was in the brig. Idiot! It all comes back clearly now; harsh words with a fellow sailor, loud voices, fists thrown, and me getting my butt handed to me. Now that I think of it, that "fellow sailor" was in fact a superior officer. Cap's second!, I think to myself, How could I deck the second?!

I yell for the guard, but receive no answer. "Guard! Come on, man!", I say desperately, "There's a fight happening! I gotta get out there! The cap knows I'm the best gunner this side of the Hyades!" I give up, when I hear something coming from outside the metal room.
